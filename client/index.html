How to Use the Showing Scheduler

  1. Prepare a CSV file with columns: name, phone, preferred_time, showing_address.
  2. Click "Choose File" and "Upload Leads" to import your leads.
  3. Click "Start Calls" to initiate outbound calls and schedule showings.
  4. View confirmed showings in the "Scheduled Showings" table.
  5. Check your Google Calendar for synced appointments (coming soon).
